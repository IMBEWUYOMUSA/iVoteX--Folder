Abstract

As citizens of the new era no one can despute the importance of taking to the poll,wheather it be for local,provincial or even national governmance,the responsiblity of voting can not be questioned.our history as nation and a people is shaped by the men and women that dispite all odds took to the polls to casted their votes and ever since then nothing has never really been the same.


Introduction

Generation after generations the power of ex has been a huge part of our lives from ushering in our first elected presidence Mr Nelson Mandela to ending the carreer of one the most prolific figures in the politic space by a vote of no confidence,Mr Jacob Zuma the power of ex can not be second guested.However given the rise of the technological era one has to ask the question, is the way we vote still effective and relevant to the new generation?


Problem Background

Statics has proven that the youth has to an alarming degree abandoned the responsibility to vote, this lack of interest is fuelled by various factors such as high university fees and unemployment rates in the country just to mention a few. The purpose of this website is to re-inspire and educate the public about the importance of voting but most of all it is to provide a platform where everyone can give his country a chance to serve them by voting for their preferred leader into power. With the rise of artificial intelligence ,one cannot avoid ask themselves the question, why are we still using old and outdated way to cast in our votes and why is so little education around the subject of voting especial when it comes to why you need to vote?

Problem Statement

At iVoteX we intend to answer such questions and more by developning a website that will meet our target demand in the most efficient and innovative way possible and once and for all do away with long exhausting ques that are a form of oppression.

Research Objective

iVotex is digital web space that is aimed at revolutionising the voting world forever. we aim to bring about an innovative change in the political space by the taking the polls to the internet. Our website consists of four pages namely:
  • Home
  • Registration
  • Education
  • About us

    • Product Features and functions

    The Home page contains a detailed background of how the idea come about and what we intend of achieving with our website.
    The registration page deals with voter information ,this is the page where the voter can fill in their personal information for authentication and validation ,in this page the voter can also take an informative survey so they can access exactly which party do they need to vote for .
    TheVote page is where the digital ballot paper is located ,in this page the voter can cast in the vote by way of selecting the favourite or preferred political party, here the voter will find three ballot namely ,the local, provincial and national ballot from here the voter can vote.
    The education page is intended at informing the voter with most relevant information about their potential leader. The content of this reflects on the political history of voting and give electoral candidates an intimate look and reviews their recent updates. This is intended at giving the voter more info on their choice.
    TheAbout us page investigates the academic background and record of the founder of the webpage. It explores the profile and interests of the developer and share his contact information for the public interested in his services.
